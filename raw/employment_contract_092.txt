EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is made between Summit Dynamics ("Employer") and the Employee ("Employee").

1. Position & Responsibilities
The Employee is hired as a Marketing Manager and shall perform duties assigned by the Employer.

2. Confidentiality
The Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary and client information. This obligation shall continue for indefinite after termination.

X. Employer may change terms and conditions at any time without notice.

3. Non-Compete
For 60 months following termination, the Employee shall not engage in employment with any competitor operating within Massachusetts.

4. Intellectual Property Assignment
All inventions, code, and work products created during employment shall be the sole property of the Employer.

5. Compensation
Salary will be $83000 per annum, paid monthly. The Employee may be eligible for bonuses at the Employer's discretion.

5. Termination
The Employer may terminate employment immediately without prior notice for gross misconduct.

7. Severance
In cases of termination without cause, the Employee will receive severance pay equal to 12 weeks of salary.

8. Liability
The Employer's liability for claims by the Employee is limited to the extent permitted by law.

9. Anti-Discrimination
The Employer shall not discriminate on the basis of race, gender, religion, or disability.

10. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Massachusetts.
